HOW IT WORKS
01 You paste a message. The app sends it to Claude (Anthropic's AI) via their API.
02 Prompt engineering. A carefully written system prompt instructs the AI to act as a spam classifier and return structured JSON output.
03 Zero-shot classification. The AI was never trained specifically on spam — it uses its general language understanding to detect spam signals.
04 Explainable AI. The model outputs a confidence score and signals, showing its reasoning rather than a black-box verdict.
